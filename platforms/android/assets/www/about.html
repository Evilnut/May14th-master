About Us

Connect-Ed is a free app that seeks to provide students at institutions across the country with a way to better enjoy their time as students through socially interactive features and mobile games with actual,tangible prizes for achievement. Our vision is to be the only app a student needs at school, by providing academic, course and campus information, as well as the opportunity to interact with other students through games, achievements, leader boards.

Connect-Ed is developed by Evilnut Creative Technology, a Vancouver based start-up founded by local graduates with a passion for technology and improving the student life experience. For more information, please visit http://www.evilnut.ca/

Privacy Policy: Connect-Ed does not collect, store or transmit any private user information such as IDs, Passwords, emails or other personal Information. If you have any questions, you can contact us at service@evilnut.ca
